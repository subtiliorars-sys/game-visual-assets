# Gaps that cannot (or should not) be filled with free third-party assets

Policy: **CC0 / public-domain only** for third-party vendor packs.
When free sources do not exist — or would violate trademark / brand / privacy —
we create **house originals** under `original/jimmythehat/`.

## Why free sourcing stops here

| Need | Why not free | House stand-in | Still needs artist/composer |
|------|--------------|----------------|-----------------------------|
| **Jimmy the Hat** character / logo | Brand IP; no free pack can be “our” mascot | `brand_jimmy_hat_64.png`, `jimmy_hat.obj` | Finished character sheet, animations, marketing renders |
| **Ninefold** virtue / petal motifs | Game-specific mythology | `brand_ninefold_petal_32.png` | Full 9-virtue icon set, glyph language |
| **WhiteWhale / Cetacea** whale mark | Distinctive house brand | `brand_whale_mark_64.png`, `whale_mark.obj` | Hand-crafted brand lockup |
| **Whale / nature audio** | Real whale songs & field recordings are usually copyrighted or licensed; we refuse to scrape them | `prop_whale_call_synth.wav` (tones only) | Commissioned synth pad / licensed library |
| **Title cards & store art** | Steam/itch pages need unique marketing | stubs only | Illustrator pass per title |
| **Voice acting / dialogue** | Kenney VO pack is generic letters/numbers; not our copy | use Kenney `voiceover-pack` for UI only | Cast sessions |
| **Full original soundtracks** | Free jingles ≠ thematic OST | Kenney `music-jingles` for prototypes | Composer per game |
| **Exact sports sims (bocce lawn, curling ice realism)** | Sports-pack is generic; lawn/ice photo textures often NC | `prop_bocce_*` + Kenney sports | Photogrammetry or commissioned mats |
| **Academy / Ilerioluwa brand** | Private vault — never vendor into games | n/a | Stay in `Ilerioluwa-Media` |
| **DrivingMeNuts sugar-nuts fantasy car skins** | Brand flavor beyond Kenney car-kit | car-kit + house paint later | Unique liveries |
